Business Investment Visa for Canada and
Investor Opportunities

The Business Investment Visa for Canada is for entrepreneurs and investors who want to start, buy or invest in a business in Canada. Qualified applicants have the opportunity to participate in economic growth and pursue long-term immigration options through different federal and provincial pathways that fit their business goals..

Investment Eligibility Assessment

Understand business requirements, investment expectations, and immigration criteria before selecting the most suitable pathway.

Strategic Investment Planning

Investigate the various options for investment and business structures based on your immigration goals and future plans.

SERVICE ELIGIBILITY

Canada Investment Visa Requirements for Eligible Applicants

Investment Capacity

Demonstrate sufficient financial resources to make a qualifying investment and support business activities in Canada.

Business Experience

Applicants should possess at least two years of business ownership, management, or entrepreneurial experience.

Language Proficiency

Have a basic level of English or French language proficiency to enable business operations and integration.

Financial & Banking Profile

Provide verifiable business credentials, financial records, and a strong banking and transaction history.

Medical & Background Assessment

Pass medical tests and perform assessments as necessary required by immigration officials.

Business Plan & Intent

Make a clear business proposal that defines objectives of investment, operational plans and economic contributions.
