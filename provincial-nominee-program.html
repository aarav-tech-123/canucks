ABOUT THE PROGRAM

Provincial Nominee Program for Canadian Immigration Pathways

The Provincial Nominee Program enables the Canadian provinces and territories to nominate qualified people according to the local economic and labor market needs. The Canadian Provincial Nominee Program offers a unique chance to skilled employees, entrepreneurs, and graduates to seek permanent residency by a province-specific immigration streams.

Province Selection Guidance

Determine appropriate provincial pathways depending on the qualifications, experience and immigration goals.

PNP Application Support

Receive guidance throughout the nomination and permanent residency application process.

ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS

Provincial Nominee Program Eligibility Requirements

Work Experience

Demonstrate relevant work experience that aligns with the requirements of a provincial stream.

Educational Qualifications

Present educational qualifications that support your eligibility in the chosen nomination schemes.

Language Proficiency

Meet English or French language standards set by the participating provinces.

Settlement Intent

Show a genuine intention to live, work, and contribute in the nominating province.

Financial Readiness

Evidence of availability of adequate resources to facilitate settlement and integration into Canadian communities.

Program-Specific Criteria

Meet additional Provincial Nominee Program Eligibility requirements established by individual provinces.
