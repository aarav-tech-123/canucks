BENEFITS OF EXPRESS ENTRY

Key Advantages of the Canada Express Entry Program

The Express Entry system provides skilled workers with an organized and effective channel to seek opportunities of gaining permanent residency in Canada.

  • Benefit 01

    Faster Processing Times

    Applications that qualify find their way easily than many of the traditional immigration pathways.

  • Benefit 02

    Transparent Selection System

    Applicants are ranked through a points-based system with clearly defined selection criteria.

  • Benefit 03

    Access to Multiple Programs

    Applicants can be eligible under different federal economic immigration streams administered under Express Entry.

Frequently Asked
Questions

Canada Express Entry is a online immigration system that processes the application of skilled workers to be given permanent residence. The ranking of the candidates is done according to their age, education, language skills and work experience.

Generally, the Canada Express Entry has such requirements as eligible work experience, language proficiency, educational credentials, and meeting the criteria of a qualifying immigration program that is operated by the Express Entry system.

The CRS Canada Express Entry Calculator helps calculate your Comprehensive Ranking System score depending on your age, education, work experience, language skills, and other aspects that help rank the candidates.

Some applicants might be required to show that they can settle in Canada by providing Canada Express Entry funds. This will depend on the size of the family and eligibility to the immigration program.

The Canada Express Entry latest draw refers to the most recent round of invitations issued by Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada (IRCC), including the CRS cut-off score and number of invitations.
